Maker Space

The Maker Space has been designed as an environment to support Co-Curricular activities for students in IT, Engineering, Industrial, Mechanical, Civil, GOS, and Business Programs. The opportunities and experiences in the maker space will allow students to apply their knowledge, skills and abilities in real-world experiences that relate to their college and career interests.

Hacker Space

The Hacker Space is a full penetration testing lab that is designed to facilitate learning about the practical side of vulnerability assessments and penetration testing in a safe environment. The lab will make extensive use virtualization to provide vulnerable and misconfigured Windows and Linux hosts, domain controllers, web servers, mail servers, firewalls, web applications and many more systems, services and applications.
