Clubs

Maker Club

The Maker Club facilitates co-Curricular activities for students in the IT, Engineering, Industrial, Mechanical, Civil, GIS, and Business programs.

Code Club

The Code Club promotes and nurtures knowledge and understanding of programming and coding through practice and mentoring.

Cybersecurity Club

The Cybersecurity Club encourages interest in cybersecurity and promote participation in cyber events and competitions.

Computer Club

The Computer Club provides real-world PC repair and desktop support experience for club members interested in the IT field of study. The student gains experience by refurbishing, repairing, and troubleshooting donated computer systems.
